East Delhi support notes.

SHELTER: Preet Vihar Shelter Referral Desk | Preet Vihar | Ask for nearest shelter and food support referral.
SHELTER: Mayur Vihar Night Stay Point | Mayur Vihar | Useful for emergency stay guidance.
SHELTER: Laxmi Nagar Help Counter | Laxmi Nagar | Ask for temporary shelter and document guidance.

Housing and shelter queries should include current living area, family size, and document availability. District support staff may guide users toward housing schemes, shelter points, or welfare counters.

For food support, users may ask about ration eligibility, local food counters, and required identity documents.
