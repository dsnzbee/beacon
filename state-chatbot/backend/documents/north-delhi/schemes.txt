North Delhi Resource Guide
Government Schemes  
Shelter for Urban Homeless (Day-NULM): 24×7 daytime shelters run by DUSIB for urban homeless in Delhi (food, beds, basic services).  
Old Age Pension (Delhi Old Age Assistance): Delhi residents ≥60 (or ≥70 for higher aid) without means get monthly pension (≈₹2,000–₹2,500).  
Disability Pension: Delhi residents (40%+ disability, income ≤₹1 lakh) receive ₹2,500/month under Delhi’s disability pension scheme.  
Delhi Family Benefit Scheme: One-time cash grant for dependents of a deceased primary earner (age 18–60, income ≤₹1 lakh) to tide them over hardship.  
Shelters & Helplines  
Night Shelters (DUSIB): DUSIB operates rain-basera night shelters in North Delhi (e.g. Majnu Ka Tilla/Aruna Colony, Azadpur BVK, Sarai Pipal Thala, etc.) offering free beds and meals each night. DUSIB’s 24×7 Control Room (toll-free 14461) dispatches teams to assist street-homeless. Key helplines: Women’s Helpline – 1091; Child Helpline – 1098; Police/Emergency – 100 or 112; Ambulance – 102 (108).  
Hospitals & Medical Facilities  
Acharya Shree Bhikshu Hospital (Moti Nagar, NW Delhi) – Major govt multi-speciality hospital; Tel: 011-25423011.  
Burari Hospital (Kaushik Enclave, North Delhi) – District general hospital; Tel: 011-20874505.  
General Emergencies: Dial 102 (ambulance), 100/112 (police), 108 (air ambulance) in life-threatening situations.  
Legal Aid & Support Offices  
Delhi State Legal Services Authority (DSLSA): 3rd Floor, Rouse Avenue District Court Complex, New Delhi – 110002. 24×7 legal aid helpline: 15100/1516 (Also 9870101337).  
North District Social Welfare Office (DSWO): 20-21, Gulabi Bagh Shopping Complex, Gulabi Bagh, Delhi – 110007. Helpline: 9289550131. (The DSWO processes pension/benefit applications for North Delhi.)  
e-District Portal: Apply online for certificates, pensions and welfare schemes via edistrict.delhigovt.nic.in.  
Grievances/Complaints: File grievances on the Delhi Public Grievance portal or contact local police (dial 112). For women in distress, also dial 181.
