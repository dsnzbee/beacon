Central Delhi Resource Guide
Government Schemes
Shelter for Urban Homeless (Day-NULM): Provides 24×7 community shelters for the poorest urban residents, built by Delhi’s urban development board (DUSIB). Each shelter serves 50–100 persons.
Old Age Pension (Delhi Old Age Assistance): Delhi residents aged ≥60 years (with income ≤₹1 lakh/yr) receive ₹2,000 per month (age 60–69) or ₹2,500 (age 70+) as pension.
Disability Pension: Persons with ≥40% disability and low income (≤₹1 lakh/yr) get ₹2,500 per month under Delhi’s financial assistance scheme.
Delhi Family Benefit Scheme: One-time cash aid for families of a deceased primary breadwinner (age 18–60) with annual income ≤₹1 lakh.
Shelters & Helplines
Night Shelters (DUSIB): Delhi Urban Shelter Improvement Board (DUSIB) runs many night shelters. Example locations in Central Delhi include Chandni Chowk (near Fountain Chowk; contact Mrs. Paramjit Kaur, 9312668807), Nizamuddin (near Hazrat Nizamuddin Dargah; Dr. Rajesh, 9891268872), Regarpura, Karol Bagh (near 100-quarters; Paramjit Kaur, 9312668807), Mori Gate (Phool Mandi) (opp. railway colony; Indu Prakash, 9911362925), Chabi Ganj, Kashmere Gate (Ram Bazar; Paramjit Kaur, 9312668807), etc. These shelters provide food, beds, and basic amenities at night.
Control Room & Hotlines: The DUSIB Night Shelter Control Room answers emergencies at 011-2337-8789 or 011-2337-0560. Delhi’s “Rain Basera” rescue service can be reached by giving a missed call to 8826400500, which will dispatch help to homeless persons on the streets.
Hospitals & Medical Facilities
Lok Nayak Hospital (LNJP), Civil Lines: Major government multi-specialty hospital. Emergency/EPABX contact: 011-23233400. Offers trauma, medicine, surgery, etc.
Pt. Madan Mohan Malviya Hospital (Malviya Nagar): Government multi-specialty hospital. Emergency contact: 011-20867268. Provides emergency care, surgery, obstetrics, etc.
BLK-Max Super Speciality Hospital, Pusa Road: Large private hospital with 24×7 emergency. Emergency helpline: 011-4055 4055. (Also: Major centres like AIIMS, Apollo, and Fortis Indraprastha are nearby.)
General Emergencies: Dial 102 for ambulance; 100/112 for police; 1091 for women’s helpline; 108 for medical emergencies.
Legal Aid & Support Offices
Delhi State Legal Services Authority (DSLSA): Provides free legal aid to the disadvantaged. Central office at Rouse Avenue Court Complex, New Delhi – 110002 (High Court premises).
Central District Social Welfare Office: GLNS Complex, Delhi Gate, New Delhi – 110001. Handles scheme applications/grievances; helpline 011-9289550125.
e-District Portal: Apply online for certificates and welfare schemes at edistrict.delhigovt.nic.in.
Complaint/Helpline: Delhi Public Grievance Portal and local police stations handle complaints. For women’s issues, contact Delhi Commission for Women (helpline 181). For all emergencies and help, dial 112.
Sources: Official GNCTD websites and documents.
