EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: West Delhi resources include govt. welfare schemes (for elderly, disabled, women, homeless, etc.), shelters (night/temporary), hospitals/clinics, police districts and courts, and document help centers. Key contacts include district offices and helplines for emergencies and legal aid.

Users asking about schemes should first identify whether they need shelter, food, housing, legal help, or document support. For legal information, users can ask about complaint filing, basic rights, and where to seek official help.

SCHEME: SMILE (Support for Marginalized Individuals) | Rehabilitation/skill training for persons engaged in begging and transgender welfare; apply via Delhi Social Welfare Dept (Pragati Bhawan) or socialjustice.gov.in.
SCHEME: SUH (Shelter for Urban Homeless) | 24×7 community shelters (50–100 beds) for poorest urban homeless; managed by DUSIB (Delhi Urban Shelter Improvement Board). Contact: DUSIB, Vikas Bhawan-II (Bela Road).
SCHEME: OLD AGE PENSION | Monthly assistance to destitute seniors (60+) with low income; apply via Delhi e-District portal or local District Social Welfare Office. Aid: ₹2,000–2,500/month (plus ₹500 for SC/ST).
SCHEME: DISABILITY PENSION | Monthly aid to persons (0–59 yrs) with ≥40% disability; apply via e-District or Welfare Office. (Income ≤₹1 lakh, Aadhaar-linked bank account required).
SCHEME: WIDOW’S PENSION (Women in Distress) | ₹2,500/mo to eligible widows/divorced/separated Delhi women (18+ yrs, income ≤₹1L); apply via WCD Delhi (visit Janakpuri or local WEC office).
SCHEME: LADLI (Lakhpati Bitiya) | (If including girl child saving scheme) – State scheme for girl education (link via WCD). N/A on citations for brevity.
